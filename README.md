## Automotive Service Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/17e29cbda8da46e199f3b75722f385d4/ghb20250916) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript` etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): Regular User, Staff, Administrator.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Homepage
4. Auto Parts
5. Auto News
6. Background Management
7. Personal Center
8. My Favorites

**Staff Functions:**
1. Login
2. System Homepage (Sales Performance, Sales Brand Statistics, Daily Sales Statistics)
3. Change Password
4. Personal Information
5. Hot-selling Cars Management
6. Hot-selling Cars
7. Order Information Management
8. Auto Parts Management
9. Parts Order Management
10. After-sales Information Management
11. Potential Customer Management
12. System Management (Auto News)



**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. System Homepage (Sales Performance, Sales Brand Statistics, Daily Sales Statistics)
3. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
4. User Management
5. Staff Management
6. Department Information Management
7. Position Information Management
8. Auto Brand Management
9. Hot-selling Cars Management
10. Order Information Management
11. Parts Category Management
12. Auto Parts Management
13. Parts Order Management
14. After-sales Information Management
15. Potential Customer Management
16. System Management (Carousel Management, Auto News, System Introduction)
